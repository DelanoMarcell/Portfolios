    Why do you need your own website?

    Let's explore why having your own website is a game-changer in today's interconnected world.

    Connect with Ease

    It provides a central platform for people to easily reach out to you, increasing the chances of valuable connections and opportunities.

    Boost Your Online Presence

    Your own website gives you the opportunity to showcase your work, projects, and portfolio. You can provide examples of your past projects, highlight your skills, and demonstrate your expertise. This allows you to make a strong impression on visitors and differentiate yourself from others in your field.

    Take Control of Your Brand

    Having a personal website allows you to take control of your online image and branding. You can shape the narrative about yourself, present yourself in a professional manner, and ensure that the information available about you is accurate and compelling. It gives you a platform to showcase your personal brand and create a positive online presence.

    How does this platform work?

    Getting your website up and running is easy, read the steps below.

    1. Choose a Template:

    Browse through our selection of templates. If you have a custom design in mind, let us know, and we'll work together to bring your vision to life.

    2. Select a Name:

    Well obviously your website needs a name! Decide on a name for your site, ideally using your name and surname. Please note that the name you choose is not guaranteed. In the case where its not available, we will notify you and assist in choosing an alternative name that suits your brand.

    3. Share Your Details:

    Fill out the form below to get in touch with us. We'll respond promptly, and you can provide your choice of template, website name, and all relevant details we need to get your site started.

    4. Make Payment:

    Once you've provided the necessary details, you will receive banking details to make the payment. Send us the proof of payment and your site will be live as soon as we are done with it.

    5.Almost ready!

    We'll ensure your site is up and running within 2-3 business days, and in case of any delays, we'll promptly communicate with you.

    Refund Policy and Maintenance:

    1. Refund Policy: If you are unhappy with the site after development, we offer a full refund.
    2. Maintenance: Once your site is live, we provide ongoing maintenance services at a minimal cost of R35 per update.
