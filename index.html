    How does this platform work?

    Getting your website up and running is easy, read the steps below.

    1. Choose a Template:

    Browse through our selection of templates. If you have a custom design in mind, let us know, and we'll work together to bring your vision to life.

    2. Select a Name:

    Well obviously your website needs a name! Decide on a name for your site, ideally using your name and surname. Please note that the name you choose is not guaranteed. In the case where its not available, we will notify you and assist in choosing an alternative name that suits your brand.

    3. Share Your Details:

    Fill out the form below to get in touch with us. We'll send you an email on how to proceed, and once you receive an email from us you can provide your choice of template, website name, and all relevant details we need to get your site started.

    4. Make Payment:

    Once you've provided the necessary details, a link will be sent to your email address where you can make payment. We use PayFast for simple and secure payments.

    5.Almost ready!

    Your site will be live in 2-3 business days. We'll communicate promptly if any delays occur.

    Refund Policy and Maintenance:

    1. Refund Policy: If you are unhappy with the site after development, we offer a full refund.
    2. Maintenance: Once your site is live, we provide ongoing maintenance services at a minimal cost of R75 per update.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Explore our frequently asked questions below to find answers to common inquiries. We hope you'll find the information you're looking for. If your question is not addressed, please don't hesitate to reach out to us directly. We're here to assist you.

    • What can I display on my website?

      Here are some key elements you can include on your website, but not limited to:

      1. About Me: Provide a brief introduction highlighting your background, education, career objectives, key skills, and expertise.
      2. Work Experience: List previous employment history with concise descriptions of roles, responsibilities, and notable achievements.
      3. Projects: Showcase your best work with examples of projects, assignments, or initiatives, along with outcomes achieved. Include visuals like images, videos, or links.
      4. Skills: Highlight relevant skills.
      5. Education: Include details about degrees earned, institutions attended, and academic honors or achievements.
      6. Certifications and Training: Showcase relevant certifications and completed training programs to demonstrate professional development.
      7. Contact Information: Display your social media profiles for easy contact.
